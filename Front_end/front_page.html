About

Trivia Quiz

The Trivia Quiz is an interactive web application designed to provide users with a fun and engaging trivia experience. Users can answer a series of trivia questions, select their answers from multiple choices, and receive instant feedback based on their selections. The trivia questions are categorized into three difficulty levels—Easy, Medium, and Hard—to ensure that players of all knowledge levels can enjoy the game.

  • Questions Retrieval.
  • Different Difficulty Levels.
  • User Interface.
  • Dynamic Question Display.
  • Feedback System.

The main objective of this project is to provide a fun and educational experience for users by offering an easy-to-use trivia quiz platform. It serves as both an engaging pastime and a tool for learning new facts in an entertaining format.

Engage Your Mind

Challenge your knowledge with a variety of trivia questions across different categories and difficulty levels. Improve your general knowledge in a fun, interactive way!

Learn and Play

Explore new facts and figures with every trivia question. Whether you're a history buff, science enthusiast, or pop culture aficionado, there’s something for everyone.

Interactive and Fun

The trivia game is designed to be easy to use and fun to play, with instant feedback on your answers and a score to measure your performance. Perfect for a quick break or a long trivia session.
